# Stands

<hr />
The most important thing is having a good stand. To awaken your stand, you need one
Mysterious Arrow and you mustn't have a stand. There are multiple ways to get a Mysterious
Arrow such as :

- Completing your story line quests (<span className="font-bold text-jolyne">/chapter</span>)
- Looting (<span className="font-bold text-jolyne">/loot</span>)
- Opening boxes (<span className="font-bold text-jolyne">/items use</span>)
- Voting (<span className="font-bold text-jolyne">/vote</span>)
- Or by trading with other players (<span className="font-bold text-jolyne">/trade</span>)

<Callout emoji="ℹ️" type="info">
  Trade command is only available at **level 5**
</Callout>

<br/>
  If you already have a stand and want another one, you can either use :
  - <span className="font-bold bg-jolyne">/stand remove</span> : to remove it
  - <span className="font-bold bg-jolyne">/stand store</span> : to store it.
